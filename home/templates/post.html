Escape the City: How to Plan a Perfect Getaway from the Urban Chaos

Do you feel stressed, bored, or suffocated by the city life? Do you crave for some fresh air, greenery, and adventure? Do you want to explore new places, cultures, and cuisines?

If you answered yes to any of these questions, then you need to escape the city and treat yourself to a refreshing and rejuvenating getaway. Whether you want to go solo, with your partner, or with your friends, there are plenty of options to choose from.

But how do you plan a perfect escape from the city? How do you find the best deals, destinations, and activities? How do you make the most of your time and money?

Don't worry, we've got you covered. In this blog post, we'll share with you some tips and tricks on how to escape the city and have a memorable travel experience. We'll also introduce you to some of the best travel blogs and bloggers who can inspire you with their stories and insights.

Tips and Tricks on How to Escape the City

Here are some of the things you should consider when planning your escape from the city:

  • Decide on your budget and duration. How much can you afford to spend on your getaway? How long can you take off from work or other commitments? These factors will help you narrow down your options and find the best deals.
  • Choose your destination and mode of transport. Do you want to go somewhere nearby or far away? Do you want to drive, fly, or take a train or bus? Depending on your preferences, you can find various destinations that suit your needs. For example, if you want to go somewhere close to Delhi, you can check out some of the luxury resorts near Delhi that offer a quick and comfortable getaway. Or if you want to go somewhere exotic and adventurous, you can check out some of the best travel blogs that feature destinations like Kashmir, Rajasthan, or Kerala.
  • Book your accommodation and activities. Once you've decided on your destination, you need to book your accommodation and activities in advance. This will save you time, money, and hassle. You can use online platforms like Tripoto or Booking.com to find and compare various options. You can also use travel blogs as a source of information and inspiration. For example, if you want to escape to a cabin in the woods, you can read this blog post on 4 Stress-Free Ways to Escape the City & Recharge Your Soul. Or if you want to escape to an estate in Colorado, you can read this blog post on Escape the City: The leading car leasing service in Kashmir expands to provide comprehensive tour and travel services.
  • Pack smartly and lightly. When escaping the city, you don't need to bring a lot of stuff with you. Just pack the essentials like clothes, toiletries, gadgets, documents, and money. You can also pack some snacks, books, games, or anything else that will make your trip more enjoyable. Try to avoid overpacking or underpacking as both can cause inconvenience and stress.
  • Enjoy your escape and share your experience. Finally, when you're ready to escape the city, just relax and enjoy your trip. Don't worry about work or other problems. Just focus on having fun and making memories. You can also share your experience with others by writing a blog post, posting on social media, or leaving a review. This will help others who are looking for ideas on how to escape the city.

Best Travel Blogs and Bloggers to Follow

If you need more inspiration and guidance on how to escape the city, you should follow some of the best travel blogs and bloggers in the world. These are people who have dedicated their lives to exploring new places, cultures, and experiences. They share their stories, tips, and insights through their blogs, social media, and other platforms.

Here are some of the best travel blogs and bloggers to follow in 2023:

  • Legal Nomads: Jodi Ettenberg is a former lawyer who quit her job to travel the world in 2008. She has been blogging about her adventures ever since, with a focus on food, culture, and history. She also writes about living with chronic pain and how travel helps her cope. Jodi is a master storyteller who can transport you to different places with her words and photos.
  • The Planet D: Dave and Deb are a Canadian couple who have been traveling the world since 2008. They have visited over 100 countries and all seven continents. They blog about adventure travel, couples travel, photography, and video. They also inspire others to live their dreams and push their boundaries.
  • Adventurous Kate: Kate McCulley is one of the most popular solo female travel bloggers in the world. She has traveled to over 80 countries and has had many amazing and crazy experiences along the way. She blogs about solo travel, female travel, budget travel, and offbeat destinations. She also shares her honest opinions and advice on various topics related to travel and life.
  • The Blonde Abroad: Kiersten Rich is a former corporate finance professional who left her career to travel the world in 2011. She has since visited over 70 countries and has built a successful online brand. She blogs about luxury travel, female travel, festivals, photography, and fashion. She also runs women-only tours and retreats around the world.
  • California Through My Lens: Josh McNair is a California native who loves exploring his home state. He blogs about the best places to visit in California, from national parks and beaches to cities and small towns. He also covers hiking, camping, road trips, food, and photography. He aims to inspire others to discover the beauty and diversity of California.
